It was a cold Halloween night in 1963 when little Michael Myers  killed his sister, Judith Myers in cold blood.  He was sent to Smiths Grove Sanitarium for his crime. There he stayed for 15 long years.

But on October 30, 1978, while being transferred for a court date, a 21 year old Michael Myers steals a car and heads for his home town of Haddonfield, Illinois. When there, he stalks three teenagers, Laurie Strode, Annie Brackett, and Linda.

17 yr old Laurie is the quiet type, preffering babysitting over parties. While at school, her teacher is talking about fate, while a bored Laurie looks out the window, she sees a man in a white mask looking at her. While walking home with Annie, she sees the same man looking at her from behind a hedge

That night, Annie and Laurie are babysitting across the street from each other.  Laurie with the comic loving Tommy Doyle, and Annie with the TV addicted Lindsey Wallace. While talking to Laurie on the phone, Annie spills butter on herself. when in the washroom, we see The Shape outside the door, he locks her in. After getting stuck, Lindsey frees her. Annie's boyfriend, Paul calls and he wants her to pick him up. She sends Lindsey to go to the Doyle house with Laurie.Then she goes to her car,  forgets her keys, goes back, gets in the car and gets killed.

Later on, Linda goes to the Wallace house with her boyfreind,Bob,and finds out that Lindsey and Annie are gone. Her and Bob go upstairs and,
shortly after they get killed.

Meanwhile Laurie thinks that Annie is at the Wallace house. Laurie thinks that her freinds are playing a Halloween prank on her. She goes upstairs, only to find the bodies of her freinds. she backs away and we see The Shape behind her. he  stabs her shoulder,and she falls down the stairs.

Meanwhile, Dr. Loomis is hot on his track. After Michael chases her around for a while, Dr. Loomis finds the car that Michael stole. Inside the Doyle house, Michael is choking Laurie, she pulls off his mask, Dr. Loomis suddenly appears and shoots michael 6 or 7 times. He falls off the balcony, but when Dr. Loomis looks back he is gone.
