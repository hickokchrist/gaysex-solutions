## 🍝 Grandpa Canasoda's Marinara and Meatballs


> Sauce Ingredients:
>
>
> - One onion, chopped finely
>
> - Four+ cloves garlic, minced
>
> - One tablespoon basil
>
> - One teaspoon oregan-o<br>
><small>(what the hell??)</small>
>
> - One teaspoon each, salt & pepper
>
> - One can crushed tomatoes
>
> - One can whole peeled tomatoes
>
> - One small can tomato paste
>
> - One cup water
>
> - One tablespoon red wine vinegar
>
>
> Meatball Ingredients:
>
>
> - One pound each, ground beef and Italian sausage (I like hot)
>
> - One+ clove garlic, minced
>
> - One-third cup cheap parmesan
>
> - One-third cup breadcrumbs
>
> - One large egg
>
> - One teaspoon basil
>
> - One teaspoon each, salt & pepper
>
>
> *For the sauce*, combine all ingredients into your instacrock except the
>whole peeled tomatoes. Pour the liquid from that can into the pot and
>hand-crush the tomatoes until they are pulverized. I like to remove the
>tomato anuses at this step. Stir.
>
> *For the meatballs*, combine all ingredients into a big mixing bowl.
>I've got some kind of compulsion that forces me to fold the beef and
>sausage together until there are at least a million layers, sometimes I
>do a billion. The connective tissue starts to really 'pop', in the sense
>that it erupts out of the homogeneous meat paste.
>
> *Form the meat into balls* and place them into the pot with the sauce.
>
> *Cook on low* for at least four hours, ensure the meatballs are fully
>submerged in sauce. Stir occasionally.
>
> <strong>*Enjoy with rigatoni noodles!*</strong>
