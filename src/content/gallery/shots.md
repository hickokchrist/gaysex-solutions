<p>
In college, I threw a halloween party. God spoke to me and told me to make gelatin dessert shots with little fetuses in them. The bb'z were made of fondant, with a shell candy piece in the head for crunch! And because God told me to do this, you aren't allowed to be mad at me.
</p>
<p>
Black dots of icing formed the eyes. I curled the little limbs into limp shapes, and twisted a li'l umbilical into each abdomen (as GOD intended). Each one was immersed in a slurry of cherry/strawberry gelatin dessert as well as pulverized pieces of maraschino cherry.
</p>
<p>
They were, as fondant tends to be, absolutely <i>disgusting</i>. However, the vision? It was 100% executed.
</p>
