<p>
One of my plans for Beyond Wonderland was to make some kandi bracelets with custom <a href="/floptropica" target="_blank">Floptropican</a> beads. I recalled that I totally know how to make custom polymer clay bead designs.
</p>
<p>
The first version was a good exercise, gotta dust off the old cobwebs. It's funny, at each stage, lack of material has felt like the biggest constraint. Reader, you are about to watch me make three versions of these damn beads!
</p>
<p>
V1 features include an attempt at a palm tree, and a mixture of colors as I learn in realtime how the pigments stain my fingers.
</p>
