Oh, also, gasoline is $6 per gallon because my car needs premium (<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tp8INMugpf0&t=31s" target="_blank">duuuude!</a>). 
<br><br>Replace trips to the city I did! For my second interstate bicycle trip, I wanted to document parts of it to make a helpful video.
