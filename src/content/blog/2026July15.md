<h2>Hilary Duff came to ridgefield, y'all</h2>
<p>
We got tickets to Hilary Duff for like $20, who could say no to that? While waiting to be picked up, I was feeling anxious and just stood outside to wait and make the pickup smooth. My neighbor ran into me coming home from work and I learned that he and his girlfriend play soccer! He was going to play a game that night, and did not know who Hilary Duff was.
</p>
<p>
While I wouldn't consider myself a fan of Hilary per se, I do have With Love in my libary [sic] and like to go places for the plot or for the vibes. There were more songs that I recognized than I thought - I did not grow up on diz-nee channel.
</p>
<p>
La Roux opened, which was very exciting! Except we were waiting in line for fish and chips and only got to walk out to the stage area during the last song (Bulletproof). On the bright side, I got an extra piece of fish (which I could not finish) when I was vinegaring up my box and saw they forgot all my fish.
</p>

<p>
My camera is so terrible, the telephoto lens is so bad. And we were far away, so I knew I had to get up to the front for With Love for the final choreo. She was so funny, there's a <a href="https://www.tiktok.com/@bulbouscorm/video/7662762096236449038" target="_blank">whole production</a> about that and she brings people from the audience on stage to do the dance with her.
</p>
<p>
We closed out the show, which ended up with about two solid hours of sitting in the car trying to get out of the parking lot. Last time I went to the Cascades Ampitheater, we found some back exit out of the parking lot and left without much fuss. Not so, this time! We had an opportunity to listen to my newest episode of <a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/Hole-McJail/sonic-stew-with-hole-mcjail-july-14-2026-soup-seven/" target="_blank">Sonic Stew</a>, however, as my friends were kindly eager to listen.
</p>
<p>
We all made it home safe and sound though, and it was a lovely evening. My buddies each ran into one of their friends at the concert and it was very cute.
</p>
