Ah, so the No Kings Protest. The thing about that is, I just happened to be here. Today I roke up and decided to run some errands.  

Originally, my plan was to have lunch with a friend, kill some time in Hollywood (Portland), then meet some other friends who live in the neighborhood before my DJ shadowing tonight. A full day! I brought my computer and intended to park somewhere in Portland to work on my website/blog entries/promotional audio cart. Meeting up for lunch ended up being a missed connection, instead I redirected to the Vancouver Farmer's Market and waterfront. I gave my card to an incredibly handsome guy working the Farmer's Market, and I only made it moderately awkward! Onward and upwards, as they say.

So I went to the waterfront and oops, all protests!

Some of the signs are very funny, there are a lot of people. By the time I'm at this stage in writing, the protest seems to have dissipated. Timecheck: 2:29pm. I got here just before 2pm as I made sure to swing by the cute farmer market man's stand toward closing (and, as according to plan, he wasn't helping anyone at the time hehe).
